# Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
## CSE0001 Digital Literacy | Midhun Keshav J T | 25BCE10589

## Tool Used
Canva (https://www.canva.com) – Free browser-based design platform

## Design Description
The infographic is titled "Navigate the Digital World" and covers three topics:

1. What is Digital Literacy?
   The ability to find, evaluate, create and communicate information
   using digital technologies responsibly and effectively.

2. Safe Internet Practices
   - Use strong passwords with letters, numbers and symbols
   - Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) on all accounts
   - Never click links from unknown senders
   - Avoid sharing personal information online

3. Professional Online Presence
   - Maintain a clean, professional LinkedIn profile
   - Use GitHub to showcase your coding projects
   - Think before you post – employers check social media

## Reflection
I chose Canva because it offers ready-made infographic templates that are
easy to customise without any design experience. The most challenging part
was deciding what information to include in a single page without making
it too cluttered. I learned that good design is as much about what you
leave out as what you include. The experience taught me to communicate
clearly and visually – a skill useful beyond just digital literacy.
